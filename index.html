About Us

Who we are

At Funnel Studio, we specialize in turning complex data into clear insights for small businesses. As a digital analytics company, we create custom dashboards in Looker Studio that help you track and understand key metrics across marketing, sales, and operations. From monitoring ad spend and lead generation to analyzing revenue and support activity, we bring all your data into one intuitive report—so you can make informed decisions with confidence and speed.
